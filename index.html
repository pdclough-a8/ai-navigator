AI Navigator - Overview
Quick orientation and usage tips

The AI Navigator provides a way of characteristing and viewing AI systems from diffferent perspectives to help you better understand AI. The interactive web page maps systems across three interlocking dimensions. Use the bullets below to get started or jump straight into the page.

  • Layers - what the system is (capabilities, data, algorithm).
  • Lifecycle - when concerns are most actionable (define, data, build, evaluate, deploy, operate).
  • Lenses - how to evaluate the system (ethics, security, business value, etc.).

Tip: click the i icons on lenses and layers to open related resources.
